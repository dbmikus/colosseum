What is Collosseum?

Collosseum is a place for people to come together and compete. Users can create and join arenas where they battle head to head in any competition you can imagine utilizing their mouse, their webcam, their microphone, their keyboard, and any skills they bring to the table. Spectators vote on who they want to see continue, and the loser is thrown to the lions, only to be replaced by a random spectator!
How can I play?

Using the buttons at the top of your page, you can navigate to the list of Arenas where you can immediately start spectating and making your voice heard by voting. Or, if you want to jump right in, you can create your own Arena and start out at the center of all of the fun. Good luck!
